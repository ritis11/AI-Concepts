If the data is imbalanced, we have to split it into train and test with the stratify technique, which maintains the ratio of the classes in the test sample as well as in the train sample. 
After that, we have to utilize certain techniques to either oversample the minority data or undersample the majority data. 

1. SMOTE :
   It manufactures new points by drawing a small line between the k-minority neighborhood points in feature space and placing a new point somewhere along that line.
